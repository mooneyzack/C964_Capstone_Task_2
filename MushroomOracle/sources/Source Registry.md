## Source Acquisition Priority

For systematically expanding the vault, prioritize in this order:

1. **Open-access journals** (Studies in Mycology, Persoonia, IMA Fungus, MycoKeys, Frontiers in Fungal Biology) — free, peer-reviewed, immediately usable
2. **UNITE + GenBank** — sequence data for species pages
3. **GBIF** — occurrence data for distribution maps
4. **PubMed/PMC** — free full-text articles for health/medicinal claims
5. **Key books** (Koji Alchemy, Fungal Machines, Mycelium Running) — foundational references
6. **Psychedelic Alpha newsletter** — free tier tracks the legal/clinical landscape
7. **Subscription journals** — access via institutional login or interlibrary loan as needed
8. **Paid market reports** — only when specific commercial data is needed for content
